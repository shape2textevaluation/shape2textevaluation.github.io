Comparing draping methods

In this study, we are comparing different methods for draping a virtual 3D garment over the human body. You are presented triplets of images, where we drape the same garment using 3 different methods.
Please click on the one that you find the most realistic, i.e. with the least visual defects, or the most pleasing and natural to your eyes. If you cannot make up your mind, pick the last answer.
A new draping simulation is shown after each click, as an endless stream. You can stop at any time.
