Exploring text and shape

In this study, we are exploring the fidelity of some 3D shapes with a text description. You are presented a pair of 3D objects, together with a sentence.
Please click on the object you find the most faithful to the text description, i.e. with the shape and appearance which is clearly described by the text. If you cannot make up your mind, pick the last answer.
A new simulation is shown after each click, as an endless stream. You can stop at any time.
